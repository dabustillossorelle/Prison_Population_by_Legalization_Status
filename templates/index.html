The total prison population is higher in states that prohibit marijuana for recreational or medicinal use. In states that have legalized recreational marijuana use the private prison population is strikingly low while the private prison population in states where marijuana use is prohibited is very high. There is evidence to suggest that the owners of private prisons give money to politicians who oppose marijuana legalization (Williams, 2017, Nanan, 2017). The Pharmaceutical Industry also have an intrest in stopping marijuana legalization. It is estimated that "major pharmaceutical companies stand to lose 18.5 billion dollars in profits in medical becomes legal in all 50 states (Mealey, 2018)."
